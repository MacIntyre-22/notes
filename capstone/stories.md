# Stories

## Story 1
### Take back our home

Your people have been misplaced by generic enemy. You and your people plan to take back their home. This place used to be a utopia for them. Your people have been outcasted ever since the take over. You are chosen to try and infiltrate their home, pretend to be one of them and find their weaknesses. You discover that they aren’t so bad after all and they really just needed a place to live. You have to decide to try and make peace, attack them, or join them. These could go bad or good depending on other factors like stats or choices.

#### Alternate

- You find out that your people aren’t so good after all
- Or you find out both sides have an awful past

## Story 2
### The Silent Enemy

A long-standing enemy has suddenly gone silent. No attacks, no communication, no movement. Your leaders suspect a trap and send you to investigate. As you explore their territory, you discover they are dealing with a greater internal threat. You can report back and prepare for war, assist them to prevent something worse, or use their weakness to eliminate them entirely.

#### Alternate
- The “enemy” never existed as believed
- The internal threat was created by your own people
