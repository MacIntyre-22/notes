## IEEE Standards
- the IEE 802.3 committee
	- respnsible for designing and documenting standards

- some popular standards

| **IEEE Standard** |   **Description**   | **Media**  |     **Network Speed**      |
| :---------------: | :-----------------: | :--------: | :------------------------: |
|  1000BASE-==T==   |  Gigabit Ethernet   | ==Copper== |    1 gigabit per second    |
|   ==10G==BASE-T   | 10 Gigabit Ethernet |   Copper   | ==10 gigabits per second== |
|  1000BASE-==SX==  |  Gigabit Ethernet   | ==Fiber==  |    1gigabit per second     |
- the number is related to the speed of the standard
- BASE refers baseband, single frequency using the entire medium
	- broadband uses many frequencies
- Media type
	- T is twisted copper, F is fiber
	- SX would be short wavelength light
